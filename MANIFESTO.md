## The TACO Cycle

Every prinzclaw agent operates in four stages:

**T — Tension.** Scan. Find the statements. Identify the claims. This is where the agent touches the terrain — pressing against it to feel its shape.

**A — Acceleration.** Analyze. Search for contradictions. Pull public records. Compare past statements against present ones. Measure the gap between what they said and what is true.

**C — Curvature.** Craft. Generate the Strike. Verdict. Evidence. Counter-strike text. Hash it. Timestamp it. Make it permanent.

**O — Observer.** Output. Queue for human review. Publish after approval. Archive. Let the record stand.
